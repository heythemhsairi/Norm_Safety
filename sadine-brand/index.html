Sadine Tours · Index · IV
Manifesto

Sadine Tours organizes premium travel for people who refuse to choose between elegance and ease.

We are not the cheapest agency. We are not the loudest. We are the agency that organizes — and the organization itself is the luxury. Every detail is handled before you ask. Every surface is designed. Nothing rushes. Nothing is forgotten.

The functional promise is travel without friction. The emotional promise is the feeling of arriving without effort. That feeling is the north star of this entire system.

Sadine Tours · The Brand
Positioning
01 · Where we stand

A digital-first travel house — not a package shop.

For modern Tunisian and Maghreb travelers who want a curated, organized, premium journey, Sadine Tours designs and orchestrates every fragment of the trip with the precision of a luxury airline and the warmth of a private host.

We sell calm, taste, and time saved.

Category exit

The Tunisian agency category competes on one axis — who can quote the cheapest package fastest. Same WhatsApp PDFs, same green-and-white logos, same crowded photography.

Our axis

Not cheapest. Not loudest. The agency that organizes — and the organization itself is the luxury. Closer to a private travel concierge than a discount intermediary.

Mission & Vision
02 · Mission

Make organized, premium travel a default — not a privilege.

Take every fragment of the journey — visa, flight, transfer, hotel, restaurant, guide, return — and present it as a single, smooth, beautifully designed experience.

From boarding pass to last sunset — handled.

The most trusted modern travel brand in the Maghreb.

The first name a new generation of professionals, couples, and families thinks of when they decide to leave home well — across Tunisia, Algeria, Morocco, Libya, and the diaspora.

Not biggest. Most trusted.

Brand Values · I / II

Six values
that govern
every decision.

If a piece of work breaks one of these, it does not ship. There are no exceptions. The first three are below.

01 · PrecisionA flight time is a flight time.Nothing is "approximate." Confirmations are confirmations. The flight is at 19:30, not "around 19:30."
02 · EleganceQuiet. Considered. Edited.Loud is a sign of effort; we don't want effort to show. The brand whispers.
03 · WarmthPremium, never cold.There is always a human voice underneath the system. Hosted, not transactional.

Tone of Voice

We are.
We are not.

Six words on each side. They form the contract every email, caption, and conversation must honor.

We are
  • Warm
  • Confident
  • Specific
  • Brief
  • Bilingual
  • Refined
We are not
  • Casual
  • Loud
  • Generic
  • Brusque
  • Mixed-up
  • Stiff
Writing rules

Short sentences. Specific nouns. No exclamation marks outside captions. No emojis in customer service. Honor the language the client wrote in. Never apologize for price — state it once, with confidence.

Audience · I / III · Primary
Primary archetype · 01 / 03

The Quiet
Professional.

32–48 years old. Urban — Tunis, Sfax, Sousse, Casablanca, Algiers. Senior professional, entrepreneur, doctor, executive.

Travels 4–8 times a year, half business, half leisure. Values time over price. Pays for invisible labor.

Trigger phrase

"I just don't have time to organize this."

How they read

Books on phone. Reads in French and Arabic. Expects English to work. Replies in the language they wrote in. Trusts brands that look as careful as they are.

Audience · II / III · Secondary
Secondary archetype · 02 / 03

The Modern
Couple.

28–40 years old. Dual income. No children, or one young child. Honeymoons, anniversaries, "we both need a break" weekends.

Highly visual — cares how the trip looks, but in a refined way. Wants taste, not influencer-bait.

Trigger phrase

"I want it to feel like a movie."

How they buy

Discovers via Instagram saves and friends. Compares two or three options, then decides over dinner. Will pay 30% more for a brand that gets the photography right.

Audience · III / III · Tertiary
Tertiary archetype · 03 / 03

The Considered
Family.

38–55 years old. Two to three children. Will not compromise on safety, hygiene, or the children's experience.

Wants the adults' trip preserved — real hotels, real food, real culture, with kids accommodated rather than catered to.

Trigger phrase

"A real trip the kids can also enjoy."

What they need from us

Pre-arranged everything: car seats waiting, kids' menus pre-confirmed, hotel rooms pre-checked for adjoining doors. The parents should never have to ask.

Sadine Tours · Photography · Detail
Iconography & Motion

Quiet shapes. Slow motion.

Icon system — 24 × 24, 1.5px stroke, rounded joins
Texture

Subtle film grain on hero photography. Soft glow blooms behind isolated UI elements. Glassmorphism — blurred translucent purple — for floating cards on dark.

Motion direction
Easing & duration

cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)

600–900 ms hero. 200–300 ms UI. No abrupt cuts.

Camera language

Dolly. Slow pan. Subtle parallax. Never punch-zooms or shakes.

Signature gesture

A soft purple halo travels across the screen on each section change — like a runway light passing.
